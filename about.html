About Us

CheapCheapy is a modern travel platform operated by Auchen Travel, focused on providing competitive flight, hotel, rail, and transfer booking solutions worldwide.

Our goal is to build a scalable and reliable travel booking system that connects users with the best available travel services through advanced technology and global supplier integrations.

We are currently expanding our platform to support flight APIs, hotel providers, rail systems such as Rail Europe, and multiple payment solutions including bank transfers and online payments.

CheapCheapy is designed to evolve into a full travel booking engine supporting search, booking, payment, and ticket issuance workflows.

Our Vision

To become a trusted global travel platform offering seamless booking experiences across flights, hotels, rail, and ground transportation.
